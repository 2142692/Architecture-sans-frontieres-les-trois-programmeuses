Architecture out of the ordinary...

Human, equitable and sustainable development is impossible without architecture. That's why ASFQ has been working with communities since 2007, as the official humanitarian arm of the Ordre des architectes du Québec.

Our services

International cooperation

Architectural expertise for the international cooperation projects of Canadian NGOs and their partners in developing countries

Urban solidarity

Support for community development projects aimed at urban equity, social inclusion and helping the homeless in Montreal

Native habitat

Architecture to support infrastructure projects, build capacity and alleviate the housing crisis in First Nations and Inuit communities

Climate resilience

Preventive action to adapt Quebec to flooding and assistance in the event of disaster

Circular economy

Ecological and inclusive architecture through the recovery, redistribution and reuse of building materials

Volunteer work

Opportunities for local and international volunteer action with Architecture sans frontières Québec and its partners

Architectural education

Learning and development activities to train practitioners in the bold social architecture the world needs
